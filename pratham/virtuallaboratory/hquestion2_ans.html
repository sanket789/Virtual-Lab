Using the same concepts we used in the earlier question, the amount of energy absorbed by the plate in time Δt is given by
 
1000*α*A* Δt Joules
 
According to the Stefan Boltzmann Law, the radiant flux density of the emitted electromagnetic radiation is given by
 
εσT4 – ασT4space
 
where Tspace is the ambient temperature which is zero for our case. ε like α is a surface property. Thus the net energy lost by the system due to radiation in the given time Δt is εσT4A Δt Joules
 
This would increase the temperature of the body by ΔT governed by the equation
 
M*C*ΔT = α*1000*A*Δt – ε *σ*A*T4Δt
 
Which comes from the definition of specific heat
 
Taking the limit as Δt -> 0 the above equation becomes
 
M*C*(dT/dt) = α*1000*A – ε *σ*A*T4
