Nomenclature

The six faces of the satellite are

  1. Zenith
  2. Nadir
  3. Leading
  4. Lagging
  5. Sun Side
  6. Anti Sun Side

Nadir is the face which faces the earth. Zenith is the face opposite Nadir. Leading is the face whose normal is in the direction of motion. Lagging lies opposite to leading. Sun side is the face which always faces the sun and Anti sun side is the face which never faces the sun. Also the numbers indicate in which file number the solar radiation data is stored.



Assumptions

  • The satellite is a cuboid
  • The orbit is circular
  • The normal to zenith is parallel to the line joining the the centre of the earth and the centre of the face
  • The normal to the leading face is parallel to the tangent to the orbit
  • The average solar flux is assumed to be 1353 W/m2
  • Kepler's law has been used to determine the time period of the orbit
